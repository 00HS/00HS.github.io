About Me

My name is Hyunseo Shin. I received my M.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Seoul, where I conducted research on reliable ML/NLP systems, multilingual LLM safety, and retrieval.

My research interests include LLM alignment, multilingual safety generalization, training-free model merging, dense retrieval, and causal retrieval. Recently, I have focused on understanding how model behavior can be made more reliable under realistic and challenging settings, especially in multilingual and retrieval-based systems.

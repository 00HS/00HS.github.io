About Me

My name is Hyunseo Shin. I received my M.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Seoul under the guidance of Professor Wonseok Hwang.

My research interests include LLM alignment, reliable AI, and safety. Recently, I have focused on understanding how model behavior can be made more reliable under realistic and challenging settings.
